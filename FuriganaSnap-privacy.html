Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Privacy by Design: Furigana Snap is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, track, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

Furigana Snap does not collect any data from its users. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Photos or images captured or selected by the user
  • Detected Japanese text or generated furigana
  • Personal information (such as name, email address, or contact details)
  • Device identifiers or diagnostic data
  • Location data
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Advertising or tracking data

On-Device Processing

All functionality in Furigana Snap operates entirely on-device:

  • Japanese text detection is performed locally on your device
  • Furigana generation is performed locally on your device
  • No images or text are uploaded to any servers
  • An internet connection is not required for core functionality

Data Storage

Any images, recognized text, or furigana results remain solely on your device and are fully controlled by you. Furigana Snap does not sync, back up, or transmit this data. Deleting the app will remove all associated data from your device.

Tracking

Furigana Snap does not track users across apps or websites. We do not use tracking technologies, advertising identifiers, or analytics SDKs.

Third-Party Services

Furigana Snap does not integrate with third-party services, analytics providers, advertising networks, or external data processors.

Children’s Privacy

Furigana Snap does not knowingly collect any personal data from users of any age. You must be at least 18 years old to use Furigana Snap. By using the App, you represent that you meet this requirement.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be made available within the app or on its App Store listing. Our commitment to on-device processing and zero data collection will not change.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or Furigana Snap’s privacy practices, please contact us.

Furigana Snap helps you read Japanese with confidence while keeping your data completely private.
