Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 11, 2025

Your Privacy is Our Priority: BrowseCalc does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Collection

BrowseCalc does not collect any data from users. We believe your information should remain private and under your control.

Local Storage Only

Your calculation history is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. This means:

  • Your history never leaves your device
  • We have no access to your calculation history
  • No data is uploaded to any servers
  • No analytics or tracking is performed
  • No third-party services have access to your data

Email Communication

When you choose to send feedback or report issues through the app, your device's default email client is used. Any information you choose to share in these emails is sent directly to us and is not collected or processed by the app itself.

No Internet Connection Required

BrowseCalc works completely offline. An internet connection is only needed if you choose to:

  • Send feedback emails
  • Rate the app in the App Store

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since the app doesn't collect data, any changes will likely be clarifications or additions to better explain our privacy practices.

Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Clear your calculation history at any time
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Your data is backed up with your device backups (if enabled)

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at sugrlog at icloud.com or through the app.
