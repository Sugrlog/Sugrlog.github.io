Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data shared with third parties?

No, your data remains on your device. Sugrlog doesn't require user registration, and we don't collect or share your personal information.

Does Sugrlog work offline?

Yes, most features work without an internet connection. The barcode scanner needs internet access to look up products in the database.

Do I need to pay for the app?

Sugrlog is free to download and use. Premium features are available with a one-time purchase (no subscription).

How accurate is the nutritional information?

The app uses a community-maintained database of over 2 million products. While we strive for accuracy, it's always good to verify with the product packaging.

Can I export my data?

Yes, if you upgrade to sugrlog+ you can export your data to the Apple Health app and from there to other external sources.
