Privacy Policy


Effective Date: May 3, 2025

1. Overview

Thank you for choosing Sugrlog. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your personal information remains confidential. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

  • No Account Registration: Sugrlog does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.
  • User-Generated Data: Any entries you add to the app (such as food logs or notes) are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to our servers or any third party.
  • Open Food Facts API: When you scan a barcode, Sugrlog uses the Open Food Facts API to retrieve nutritional information about products. This lookup is not associated with any specific user and does not involve sending any personal or identifying information to the API provider.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • On-Device Storage: All data you enter in Sugrlog is stored locally on your device and is not collected or stored by us.
  • API Usage: We use the Open Food Facts API solely to provide you with product nutritional information. No personal data is shared with the API provider.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • No Sharing: We do not collect or share your personal information with any third parties, except as required by law.
  • No Advertising: Sugrlog does not display third-party advertisements and does not collect data for advertising purposes.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • On-Device Data: You can delete your data at any time by uninstalling the app or using the app's built-in deletion features.
  • No Server Storage: We do not collect or store any user data on our servers.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at sugrlog at icloud.com or through the app.
