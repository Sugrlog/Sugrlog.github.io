Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

Privacy First

MRR Buddy is built with a simple philosophy: Your data is your business.

The app is designed to prioritize privacy. Personal data, Stripe information, and financial metrics are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to us or any third-party service provider.

Data Handling

We do not currently collect or have access to the following information:

  • Stripe API Keys: Your API keys are stored exclusively in your local macOS Keychain.
  • Financial Data: Your MRR, subscription counts, growth metrics, and customer information are fetched directly from Stripe and processed locally on your machine.
  • Personal Information: We do not require account creation, nor do we collect your name, email, or any identifiable information.

Analytics and Improvement

To improve the quality and performance of MRR Buddy, we may implement anonymous usage analytics in the future. This would involve collecting non-identifiable data about how the app is used (e.g., which features are accessed or crash reports) to help us diagnose issues and prioritize improvements. Any implementation of analytics will be done with privacy in mind and will not include your personal financial data or API keys.

We do not currently collect any analytics data.

Direct Connection to Stripe

MRR Buddy communicates directly with Stripe's API to fetch your data. This communication is encrypted and secured via standard HTTPS. The data received is used to display your dashboard in the menu bar and is not shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

The app currently avoids the use of third-party SDKs, tracking pixels, or advertising frameworks. The primary external communication is between the app and the official Stripe and Exchange Rate APIs for the purpose of functioning as intended.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal obligations. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any updates. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
