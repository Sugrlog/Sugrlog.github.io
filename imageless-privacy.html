Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Your Privacy Matters: Imageless is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data about you or your browsing activity.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Imageless does not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Your browsing history or website visits
  • Personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Any data from websites you visit

How Imageless Works

Imageless operates entirely locally on your device. The extension:

  • Processes web content locally in your browser
  • Applies image blocking rules without sending data externally
  • Stores preferences locally on your device only
  • Does not communicate with external servers

Data Storage

Any settings or preferences you configure are stored locally on your device using Safari's built-in storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Third-Party Services

Imageless does not integrate with, connect to, or share data with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this policy and notify users through the extension's update mechanism. However, our commitment to not collecting user data will remain unchanged.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or Imageless's privacy practices, please contact us.

This privacy policy reflects our commitment to protecting your privacy while using Imageless. We believe that media blocking should not come at the cost of your personal privacy.
